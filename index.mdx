## Introduction

Arklow helps your infrastructure learn its own capacity, protecting you from outages and overspend. This is called [Admission Control](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Admission_control).

You tell Arklow where outstanding work sits, and where it should land. Arklow watches how each destination handles its deliveries and releases work at the pace it can absorb. It slows down when a destination degrades and speeds back up as capacity returns.
